Who we are

Hi, there! We are George and Kostas, the team that brings to you the unique e-learning experience that connects passionate learners and universities from all over the world.We come from diverse backgrounds, but we share one common belief: “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world! “

Our Vision

Every individual has the potential to create change, whether in their life, their community, or the world. The transformative power of education is what unlocks that potential. Yet, access to high-quality education has been a privilege of the few. Back in 2019, we realized it was a time for a seismic shift in learning. From the tried and true to the leading edge. From “for some” to “for all.” By opening the classroom through online learning, (MOOC name) empowers millions of learners to unlock their potential and become changemakers.
